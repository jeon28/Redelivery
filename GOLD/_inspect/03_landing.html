who we are

We offer a full set of services to support your business

Touax Global Container Solutions is a leading global player in leasing and trading solutions with a fleet of nearly 350,000 teus standard dry and refrigerated containers at the end of 2025.
We are a division of Touax Group, a distinguished international company established over 170 years ago with an unmatched expertise in three key standardized sustainable transport assets: freight railcars, river barges, and containers, representing over €1.2 billion worth of assets under management.
